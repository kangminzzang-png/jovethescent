About JOVE

About

JOVE is a niche parfumerie born in Seoul. We believe that scent is not merely a product, but a medium that carries memories and stories.

Every fragrance begins from a single narrative of creation. Light and darkness, divinity and temptation, purity and fall — the most beautiful stories are born between these poles.

"In a place where memories cannot be seen,
a scent for those who choose to be remembered."

Creation Series

The Creation Series, JOVE's debut collection, is composed of two worlds.

Lion's World — The sacred world. Fragrances of light, purity, and awe — Genesis I, Breath of Eden, Hawa.

Serpent's World — The world of temptation. Coming soon — a collection exploring the boundary between fall and beauty.

Philosophy

We capture stories through scent, creating sensations meant to be remembered forever. Each bottle is finished with a volcanic stone cap and leather wrapping, embodying the primal beauty of nature.

The name JOVE derives from the ancient Roman supreme god Jupiter (Jove) — the god of sky and light who watches over all from the highest place. Our fragrances look upon the deepest stories of human existence with that same gaze.
