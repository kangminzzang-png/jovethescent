About JOVE

We capture stories through scent,

Origin

The Name JOVE

JOVE derives from the Latin word for 'God.'
Another name for Jupiter, king of the Roman gods.
The deity of sky and light — watching over all
from the highest place.

Our fragrances carry that same gaze,
looking into the deepest stories of human existence.

"What story do you want to leave behind?"
Mission

We believe in the power of the invisible

Scent cannot be seen.
Yet it is the sense that lingers deepest.

JOVE believes scent is not simply a product,
but a medium that holds memory and story.
What is not forgotten after it fades.
That is true luxury.

Creation Series
