Materials

Nature's Rarest Elements

Craft

Reverence for Materials

Each JOVE fragrance is composed of nature's most rare and noble materials. Wood, resin, spice — ingredients used in sacred rituals for thousands of years.

We honor the depth inherent in each raw material, not excessive synthesis. Creating maximum resonance with minimal composition — that is JOVE's approach to perfumery.

Object

Like an Unearthed Artifact

Design

An Eternal Object

A 50ml cylindrical glass vessel wrapped in black leather with hot-stamped embossing. The irregular natural stone cap takes a unique form, like an artifact drawn from the earth.

A cord detail inspired by David's sling. The JOVE bottle is designed not to be discarded after use, but to be kept — an object worthy of collection.
